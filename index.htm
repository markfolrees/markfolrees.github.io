About Us

Crown Green bowling
in the heart of Sale

Legh Arms Bowling Club is one of Sale's most welcoming bowling clubs, set in the grounds of the Legh Arms pub in the heart of Sale Moor. We offer a friendly, inclusive environment for players of all abilities — from complete beginners picking up a bowl for the first time to seasoned competitors.

Whether you're looking for gentle social play on a summer evening, competitive league and cup fixtures, or simply good company and a mid-game or post-game pint, you'll find it all here.

Membership

How to join us

Joining the Legh Arms Bowling Club is simple and straightforward. We welcome new members of all ages and abilities throughout the season — you don't need any experience to get started.

  1. 01

    Come and have a look

    Pop down to the Legh Arms on a Monday or Thursday evening — no commitment needed. Watch a game, have a chat, and see if the club feels right for you.

  2. 02

    Have a free taster session

    We'll lend you a set of bowls and walk you through the basics. There's no obligation and no experience necessary — just come prepared to enjoy yourself.

  3. 03

    Complete your membership form

    If you'd like to join, simply pay your annual subscription. You'll receive your membership card and club details.

  4. 04

    Get on the green!

    You're now a fully-fledged member. Join roll-ups, enter competitions, play league games — the green is yours to enjoy all season long.

The Season

When we play

Our outdoor season runs from April through to September, with something happening most weeknights and often weekends too. Here's what to expect across the year:

March

Season Opening

The green reopens and members return for the first roll-up of the year. A great time to join — everyone's finding their feet again!

April – September

League & Social Play

Mid-week league fixtures begin. Social evenings are in full swing — the long summer evenings make for perfect bowling conditions.

July – August

Club Competitions

Our internal singles and pairs competitions take place around once a month. We also hold some open competitions where anyone can play.

September

End of Season

Final league matches and competitions. The green closes around the end of the month

Close season

Green maintenance

We hold regular work parties during the close season to keep the green and surrounds tidy. Painting, woodworking, gardening - there's always something to do and it keeps us in contact over the winter.
